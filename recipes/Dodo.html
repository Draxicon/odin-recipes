Dodo AKA Fried Plantains

Home

Description

Dodo (not the bird) is a sweet treat that (depending on how you cook it)

can act as a snack or toppings for larger meals.

Dodo is simply just fried Plantains, depending on how ripe your Plantains are

you can get make a soft and sweet snack or if not too ripe you can make some crunchy, salty, spicy, plantain chips

Today we will focus on making the sweet kind of Dodo

Ingredients

  • 4 very ripe plantains
  • vegetable oil or another oil with a high smoke point

Steps

  1. Prep Pan: Fill a pan or skillet about 2 inches deep with oil and heat over medium-high flame for about 5 minutes. The oil will start "moving" when it's ready
  2. Prep Plantains: skin and cut plantain into slices about 1-inch thick
  3. Cooking: Add plantain slices to hot oil to cook (in small Batches)
  4. While cooking: flip platains every 3 to 4 minutes until both sides are golden
  5. cooling: Place cooked platains on a baking sheet (or a plate with a paper towel on it) to drain oil

Note
