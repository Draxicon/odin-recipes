Jollof Spaghetti

Description

Jollof Spaghetti is the beautiful love child between Jollof rice and Spaghetti.

By using the rich and savory flavor of the Jollof sauce and mixing it with the

comforting taste of Spaghetti one can make a passionate filling meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 large Onions
  • 5 large Plum/Roma Tomatos
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 5 Habenero peppers
  • 3 tbsp Vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp chicken bouillon (or till your ancestors smile)
  • 2 tbsp tomoto paste
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp curry powder

Steps

  1. Chop one Onion into rings and set aside
  2. finely blend the other Onion, the tomatos, the pepper and 1 tbsp of tomato paste together
  3. in a pot add 3 tbsp Vegetable oil
