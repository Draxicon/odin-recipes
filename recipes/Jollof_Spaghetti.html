Jollof Spaghetti

Home

Description

Jollof Spaghetti is the beautiful love child between Jollof rice and Spaghetti.

By using the rich and savory flavor of the Jollof sauce and mixing it with the

comforting taste of Spaghetti one can make a passionate filling meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 large Onions
  • 5 large Plum/Roma Tomatos
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 5 Habenero peppers
  • 3 tbsp Vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp chicken bouillon (or till your ancestors smile)
  • 2 tbsp tomoto paste
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Onion Rings: one Onion into rings and set aside
  2. Blending: blend the other Onion, the tomatos, the pepper and 1 tbsp of tomato paste together
  3. Heat a pot to High heat: in a pot add 3 tbsp Vegetable oil
  4. Aromatics: add in the other tbsp tomato paste and the finely chopped onions as well as the Thyme and Curry powder to the pan
  5. Blended Veggies: Add Blended vegetables as well as chicken bouillon and stir occasionally and add salt to taste
  6. Spaghetti: While stiring, boil spaghetti until it becomes soft
  7. Adding Spaghetti: When the sauce resembles a paste add in the soft spaghetti and stir until its properly mixed

Note

additionally you can add some meatballs, Baked Fish, Dodo, greenbell peppers, scallions or soft boiled eggs to add as toppings
